US Presidents Dataset

Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/thedevastator/us-presidents-dataset?resource=download

About:
The file ThrowbackDataThursday Week 8 - US Presidents.csv contains information about the US Presidents, including their numerical order, colleges attended, birth and death details, dates of taking office and leaving office, and political party affiliations

Codebook:
Number: The numerical order of each US President. (Numeric)
Colleges: Information about the colleges or universities attended by each President. (Text)
Birth City: The city where each President was born. (Text)
Birth State: The state where each President was born. (Text)
Birth Date: The date of birth for each US President. (Date)
Death City: The city where each President passed away. (Text)
Death State: The state where each President died. (Text)
Death Date: The date when each President passed away. (Date)
Left office: The date when a particular president ended their term in office. (Date)
Took office: The date when a particular president began their term as leader of the nation. (Date)
Party: The political party affiliation for every US President. (Text)
